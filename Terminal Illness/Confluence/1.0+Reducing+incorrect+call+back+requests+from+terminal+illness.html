Background - what is the problem?
Problem raised as part of OctoRipley Roadmapping session: Lack of understanding what
constitutes a terminal illness/include explanation of terminal illness so claimant understands in
gather.
The principle of the call was to provide additional support for claimants who come under the
category of terminally ill. This support is provided in a number of ways.
For example:
• by helping them complete the application online if necessary if they do not have the
strength or support to do so
• identifying that a home visit could be required if they are too weak to get through the
process of applying
• informing them of what they are to expect as a part of next steps
• informing them about the additional element they will receive as a part of their UC due to
terminal illness
• instructing them on how they can manage their account and the support we could provide
as their condition deteriorates identifying if an appointee is appropriate
• being able to explain explicit consent to allow a third party to speak/deal on their behalf
