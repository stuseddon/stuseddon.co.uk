British Gas – App

The British Gas app is used by several million British Gas customers to help manage their accounts.

The app team were tasked with a redesign of the app at the end of 2023. We took the opportunity to not only redesign the app itself, but redesign the entire design process.

The challenge

Our goal was to create a streamlined design-to-development journey, reduce duplication of screens and create a fresh new design system on Figma, utilising tokens and variables.

The outcome would be a faster and more consistent workflow, improved design consistency, and a sped up development process.

Discovery

A full audit was completed on the existing app design to identify screen duplication and component inconsistencies.

We then ran an in person session with the team to agree an approach to:

  1. designing components
  2. token naming and structure
  3. preparing a user journey
  4. what a developer handover should look like
  5. what will be included in the guideline documents
  6. agree an order for the new screen designs

Design system strategy

Accessibility, scalability, flexibility and consistency, were the main guiding principles for the new design system.

We used a modular approach, whereby everything was broken down into reusable components and patterns (eg. buttons, forms, icons, typography etc).

Results and impact

British Gas app store

The new version of the British Gas app was launched mid 2024. The new design system has not only improved our workflow, but the resulting visual refresh saw a huge uptick in App Store and Google Store ratings.

Within a few weeks, the app's rating saw an increase from 1 star to 4.5 stars on The App Store.
