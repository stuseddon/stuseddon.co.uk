Euro 2016 wall posters

The first European Championship was held in France in 1960; the grid and typographic style used in the posters are a reference to the graphic design style which was popular in this era. I've created a unique poster for each of the 24 participating nations, including specific team and player statistics.

All 24 posters are freely available as hi-res A1 (59.4 x 84.1cm) downloads.
