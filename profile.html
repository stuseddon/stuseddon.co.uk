Hello

In 1998 I started my design career at a lithographic print company in Yorkshire.

A few years later I left for the bright lights of Staffordshire University and graduated with a Graphic Design degree, and an impressive student loan debt.

After spending 8 years in London I have recently moved to the New Forest to continue my freelance career at a slightly slower pace.

I'm an advocate of clean design, clever concepts, good typography and a great user experience.
